I'm passionate about finding my own signature scent. I think perfume is a unique way of expressing yourself, and I want to help people find theirs. To start, let’s explore some of the most famous perfumes and what makes them iconic."

Le Labo Santal 33 Eau de Parfum

An iconic unisex fragrance that has gained cult status for its distinctive woody, smoky character. Launched in 2011, it was crafted by perfumer Frank Voelkl and quickly became synonymous with modern, urban sophistication.

  • Fragrance Family: Woody Aromatic
  • Top Notes: Cardamom, Violet, and Iris
  • Middle Notes: Sandalwood and Cedarwood
  • Base Notes: Leather, Papyrus, and Amber

Jo Malone English Pear & Freesia Cologne

a beloved fragrance that embodies the fresh, delicate essence of autumn. Launched in 2010 as part of Jo Malone London's collection, it has become a favorite for its light, elegant, and fruity floral profile.

  • Fragrance Family: Fruity Floral
  • Top Notes: King William Pear
  • Middle Notes: Freesia
  • Base Notes: Patchouli, Amber, and Wood

Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male

A legendary fragrance for men, first launched in 1995. Created by Francis Kurkdjian, Le Male quickly became a classic. The bottle, shaped like a male torso dressed in a sailor’s striped shirt reflects the bold, masculine yet playful nature of the fragrance.

  • Fragrance Family: Oriental Fougere
  • Top Notes: Mint, Lavender, Bergamot
  • Middle Notes: Cinnamon, Cumin, Orange Blossom
  • Base Notes: Vanilla, Tonka Bean, Sandalwood, Cedar

Dior Sauvage

is a wildly popular fragrance, introduced in 2015 and created by the legendary perfumer François Demachy for the house of Dior. Sauvage has become a modern classic for its fresh, rugged, and versatile scent, appealing to men seeking a bold, yet sophisticated fragrance.

  • Fragrance Family: Aromatic Fougere
  • Top Notes: Calabrian Bergamot, Pepper
  • Middle Notes: Sichuan Pepper, Lavender, Geranium, Vetiver
  • Base Notes: Ambroxan, Cedar, Labdanum

Baccarat Rouge 540

a luxurious and iconic fragrance from Maison Francis Kurkdjian, created by renowned perfumer Francis Kurkdjian. Launched in 2015, this scent has become a favorite among perfume enthusiasts for its unique blend of ingredients and rich, captivating character.

  • Fragrance Family: Oriental Floral
  • Top Notes: Saffron and Jasmine
  • Middle Notes: Amberwood and Ambergris
  • Base Notes: Fir Resin and Cedar

Ariana Grande Cloud Eau de Parfum

Launched in 2018 and quickly became a favorite in the celebrity fragrance market due to its unique combination of sweet and sophisticated notes. The fragrance was created by renowned perfumers Clement Gavarry and Chris Collins

  • Top notes of jasmine, pear and bergamot pair with coconut, praline and vanilla orchid
  • sweet but luxurious daytime scent - and the fun packaging is a bonus too.
